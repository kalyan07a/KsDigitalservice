Service Collection Process

For services that require in-person collection (like printed documents), please follow this process:

  • Place your order: Submit your documents through our online portal or in-person
  • Processing time: Most orders are ready within 1-2 business hours
  • Notification: You'll receive a notification when your order is ready for pickup
  • Collection window: Orders must be collected within 3 business days
  • ID required: Bring valid photo ID to collect your order

Digital Service Delivery

For our digital services, here's what you can expect:

  • Instant processing: Most digital services are completed immediately
  • Email confirmation: You'll receive confirmation of service completion
  • Secure document handling: All digital documents are handled with strict confidentiality
  • Online tracking: Track the status of your application through our portal

Why No Shipping?
