How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Directions

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how KS Digital services ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services, including the self-print service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, address (if required for a specific service).
  • Service-Specific Information: Documents uploaded for printing, details needed for online applications (e.g., Aadhar, PAN details for related services), transaction details for bill payments or ticket bookings.
  • Payment Information: Details required to process payments via our payment gateway partners (we typically do not store full card details ourselves).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website and services, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services (e.g., process print jobs, submit applications, pay bills).
  • To process transactions and send confirmations or receipts.
  • To communicate with you regarding your service requests, support needs, or important updates.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience based on usage patterns and feedback.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our platform and users.
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We value your privacy and do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors and partners who help us operate our services (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers), but only to the extent necessary for them to perform their functions.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of KS Digital Services, our users, or others.
  • For Service Fulfillment: Information required for specific services (like application details) may be submitted to the relevant government portals or service providers as part of the service you requested.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction, subject to confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction. This includes secure handling of uploaded documents for printing, which are typically processed and deleted promptly after service completion where feasible. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, provide our services, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations. Uploaded documents for printing are generally retained only for the duration needed to complete the print job.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • The right to access the information we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data (subject to legal and contractual restrictions).
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided on our Contact page.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and indicating the effective date. Your continued use of our services after changes signifies your acceptance of the revised policy.
