What is UDYAM/MSME Registration?

UDYAM Registration is a government recognition for Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in India. It replaced the earlier system of MSME registration with a simpler, online process without any registration fees.

This registration provides an official certificate that validates your business as an MSME, making you eligible for various government schemes, subsidies, and benefits.

The UDYAM registration process is completely online and paperless. Your certificate is generated instantly upon successful application.
