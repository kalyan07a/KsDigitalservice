Frequently Asked Questions

How long is an Indian passport valid?

For adults (18+ years), passports are valid for 10 years. For minors (under 18 years), passports are valid for 5 years or until they turn 18, whichever comes first.

What is the Tatkal scheme?

The Tatkal scheme is for urgent passport issuance. Under this scheme, you can get your passport within 1-3 days after your appointment, though police verification may still be required after issuance.

Can I apply for a passport without police verification?

First-time applicants must undergo police verification. For renewals, police verification may be waived if you have a previous passport with the same address and no changes in personal details.

How long does it take to get a passport?

For normal applications, it takes about 30-45 days from application submission. Tatkal applications are processed faster, usually within 1-3 weeks.
