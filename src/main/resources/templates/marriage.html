Application Process

  1. Visit the Official Portal

    Go to Telangana State Registration and Stamps Department website

  2. Fill Application Form

    Complete Form 2 for Hindu Marriage Act or Form 5 for Special Marriage Act

  3. Upload Documents

    Scan and upload all required documents

  4. Pay Fees

    Pay the applicable registration fee online

  5. Schedule Appointment

    Book an appointment slot at your local Registrar Office

  6. Appear in Person

    Both parties must appear before the Marriage Registrar on scheduled date

  7. Witness Verification

    Three witnesses must accompany you with their ID proofs

  8. Certificate Issuance

    Receive your marriage certificate after successful verification

Important Information

Key Details
  • Registration should be done within 90 days of marriage to avoid late fees
  • Both parties must be present at the Registrar's office with two witnesses
  • Minimum age: Bride must be 18+ years, Groom must be 21+ years
  • For religious marriages, register at the sub-registrar office where marriage took place

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get the marriage certificate?

After submitting all documents and appearing at the registrar office, it typically takes 15-30 days to receive your marriage certificate. The exact timeline depends on the verification process.

What is the fee for marriage registration?

The fee varies based on when you register. If registered within 30 days of marriage, the fee is ₹100. Between 30-90 days, it's ₹200. After 90 days, court permission is required with higher fees.

Can I register if one partner is from another state?

Yes, you can register in Telangana as long as the marriage took place in Telangana. If the marriage was outside Telangana, you need to register in the state where it occurred.

What if I lost my original marriage certificate?

You can apply for a duplicate certificate at the same registrar office where you originally registered. You'll need to provide an affidavit explaining the loss and pay a nominal fee.
