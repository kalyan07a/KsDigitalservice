What is a Labour License? Telangana

A Labour License, also known as the Shops and Establishment Registration, is a mandatory legal requirement for businesses operating in Telangana. It is governed by the Telangana Shops and Establishments Act, 1988 and regulated by the Labour Department of Telangana.

This license ensures that businesses comply with state labor laws regarding working hours, holidays, wages, and working conditions for employees.

Why is a Labour License Important in Telangana?

Legal Requirement

Mandatory for all commercial establishments with employees in Telangana.

Employee Protection

Ensures compliance with labor laws protecting employee rights and welfare.

Business Credibility

Enhances your business reputation and makes it easier to get loans and contracts.

Avoid Penalties

Prevents hefty fines (up to ₹10,000) and legal issues for non-compliance.

Food service establishments with staff

Note: Any business in Telangana with 10 or more employees must obtain this license within 30 days of starting operations.

Key Requirements in Telangana

Working Hours
  • Maximum 9 hours per day
  • Maximum 48 hours per week
  • Rest interval of at least 30 minutes
Holidays & Leave
  • Weekly holiday (at least 1 day per week)
  • 10 paid public holidays
  • 12 days of casual leave annually

Application Process in Telangana

  1. Determine Applicability

    Confirm if your business requires registration based on employee count and nature.

  2. Prepare Documents

    Collect all required documents as per Telangana Labour Department.

  3. Submit Application

    Complete Form A on the Telangana Labour Department portal.

  4. Inspection (if required)

    Labour officials may inspect your premises.

  5. License Issuance

    Receive your Labour License within 15-20 working days.

Documents Required for Telangana

Business Documents
  • Business registration proof
  • Address proof of establishment
  • PAN card of business/owner
  • List of employees with details
Owner/Partner Documents
  • ID proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID)
  • Address proof of owner/partners
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Partnership deed (if applicable)

Validity and Renewal in Telangana

Validity Period

The Labour License is valid for 1 to 5 years as selected during application.

Renewal Process

Must be renewed 30 days before expiry. Renewal fees vary based on number of employees.

Penalties for Non-Compliance

Operating Without License

Fine up to ₹10,000 and possible business closure

Working Hours Violation

Fine up to ₹5,000 per violation

Leave Policy Violation

Fine up to ₹3,000 per violation
